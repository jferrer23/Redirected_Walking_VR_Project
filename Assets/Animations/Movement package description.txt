Movement Package

This package features a collection of fbx animations for a bipedal humanoid character walking, running and performing other general movement actions

Walking 1: Standard walking animation

Walking 2: Alternate Standard walking animation

Cautious Walk: Walking cautiously forward

Nervous Walk: Walking forward nervously

Drop to Knees: Dropping to knees dramatically

Running 1: Running back and forth

Running 2: Alternate running back and forth

Running 3: Standard running animation

Running 4: Alternate standard running animation

Running Stop: Coming to an abrupt stop while running

Standing Turns Left: Left stationary turns at 90, 135 and 180 degrees

Standing Turns Right: Right stationary turns at 90, 135 and 180 degrees

Turn and Walk Left 1: Turning left at 90 degrees and walking

Turn and Walk Left 2: Turning left at 135 degrees and walking

Turn and Walk Left 3: Turning left at 180 degrees and walking

Turn and Walk Right 1: Turning right at 90 degrees and walking

Turn and Walk Right 2: Turning right at 135 degrees and walking

Turn and Walk Right 3: Turning right at 180 degrees and walking

Wade Through Water 1: Wading through knee deep water

Wade Through Water 2: Wading through waist deep water

Walk and Look Around 1: Observing environment while walking

Walk and Look Around 2: Alternate observing environment while walking

Walking Across Beam: Walking across a thin beam

Walking with Broom 1: Walking while holding a broom

Walking with Broom 2: Alternate walking while holding broom

Walking with object: Walking while holding an object
